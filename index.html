An NFT is a unit of data stored in a digital record, called a blockchain, that can be sold and traded. An NFT can be linked to a specific digital or physical asset (such as a file or physical object) and a license to use the asset for a specific purpose. An NFT (and the associated license to use, copy or display the underlying asset) can be traded and sold on digital markets.
